About Us

CEO

My name is Mark Zuckerberg (born May 14, 1984). I am an American technology entrepreneur and philanthropist. I am known for co-founding and leading AwesomeCompany as its chairman and chief executive officer. I have also co-founded and is a board member of the solar sail spacecraft development project Breakthrough Starshot.

CTO

My name is Steve Jobs (born on February 24, 1955).I am an American business magnate, entrepreneur, industrial designer, investor, and media proprietor. I am the chairman, chief technological officer (CTO) of AwesomeCompany, and co-founder of Apple Inc., the chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar, a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar, and the founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT.

COO

My name is Bill Gates (born October 28, 1955).I am an American business magnate, investor, author, philanthropist, and humanitarian. I am best known as the principal founder of Microsoft Corporation. I also hold the positions of chairman, COO and chief software architect of AwesomeCompany, while also being the largest individual shareholder until May 2014.
